For Teachers

Open up the world of pathology

Our new competition is aimed at children from primary age to sixth form. The different levels, from Mild to Super-charger mean that it is accessible to all and presents challenge to older pupils as they move to the higher levels.

Certificates are available to all participants and the top three scorers in each age category win prizes.

The competition demonstrates what pathologists do, and also shows the significant advances being made in the area as pathology becomes more and more digitally based.

Significant projects worldwide are developing algorithms and artificial intelligence applications to help pathologists diagnose more accurately and faster, for the benefit of the patient.
